Instructions

  • Neptune Portals
    • Use the buttons to open all portals at once or with delay.
    • Use the Select State dropdown to open the portal for an individual state.
  • KnowledgeBase Portals
    • Use one of the five dropdowns: Script, KB, Map, Script + KB, or Script + KB + Map.
    • Select a state from the dropdown to open the corresponding page(s).
    • Only one dropdown can be open at a time.
    • The Script + KB and Script + KB + Map options also display the call intro script below.
  • Search Tyler KB Portal
    • Enter keywords or phrases into the search box.
    • Click Search to open a new tab with results from the Tyler KB portal.
    • Supports spaces and special characters automatically.
  • Popup Blockers
    • Ensure popups are allowed or the links will not open correctly.
  • Performance
    • If your device has low memory, opening many tabs at once might slow down your browser.
